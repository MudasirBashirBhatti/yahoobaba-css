counter increment or list numbers custom styling

    there are two counter related properties and one function.All counter properties are inter connected.
  1. counter-reset
  2. counter( )
  3. counter-increment
1.counter-reset: my_chapter 1 ;
2.counter( ): content:counter(my_chapter) ;
3.counter-increment: my_chapter 5;
content: "Chapter " counter(my_counter , upper-alpha) " : ";
content: "Chapter " counter(my_counter , upper-roman) " : ";
content: "Chapter " counter(my_counter , upper-latin) " : ";
content: "Chapter " counter(my_counter , lower-latin) " : ";
content: "Chapter " counter(my_counter , lower-roman) " : ";
content: "Chapter " counter(my_counter , lower-alpha) " : ";
